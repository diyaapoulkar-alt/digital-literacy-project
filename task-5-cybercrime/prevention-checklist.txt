Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
2. Do not approve unknown payment requests
3. Always verify the sender before making transactions
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links
5. Use strong passwords and enable app security
6. Regularly check bank transaction history
7. Install apps only from trusted sources
8. Log out from shared devices

Financial/UPI Safety:
9. Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering PIN
10. Double-check UPI ID before sending money
11. Do not accept payment requests from unknown people

Reporting:
Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
